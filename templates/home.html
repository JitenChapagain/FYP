Blood Donation Facts
  • 1 donation can save up to 3 lives.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50kg
  • No major illness in the last 6 months
  • Not currently pregnant
  • Healthy hemoglobin levels
